How it works

  • 3 innings, head to head. One of you is randomly the home team.
  • Each at-bat you get a team + position (P, C, 1B, 2B, 3B, SS, LF, CF, RF, in order). Name any current player at that spot.
  • Bat through all 9 spots: that's a run, and the order turns over with fresh teams.
  • You get 3 swings at each spot — name any player who fits and you're on. Whiff on all three and it's an out, but you stay in the order and keep batting. 3 outs (or 3 runs) ends your half.
  • No clock. Take all the time you want.
  • Bad call? You can overrule the ump on a miss — honor system, and your opponent sees exactly what you typed.
  • Each half-inning draws its own teams, so you can watch your opponent's at-bats live — gameday style — without spoiling your own.
  • Tied after 3? Extra innings.
